Project 1 1 Examine Data Breaches Visual

Security Awareness coursework. August 21, 2025.

Summary

I examined the data breach involving the Teachers Insurance and Annuity Association, which occurred in May 2023. The incident impacted about 2.3 million people and was connected to MOVEit, a widely used file transfer platform. Exposed information included full names, Social Security numbers, addresses, and phone numbers.

Building the visualization helped me connect the technical details to the real impact on individuals. It reinforced that cybersecurity is not only about systems and policies. It is about protecting people and their peace of mind.

This project also made me reflect on my own digital habits and how I share and store personal information.

Skills demonstrated

  • Clear written communication for non technical audiences
  • Security awareness and personal risk reflection
  • Connecting technical events to real world consequences

Works cited

Goodin, Dan. Ars Technica. May 22, 2023.
